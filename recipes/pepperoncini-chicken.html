Creamy Pepperoncini Chicken

Description

A quick delicious meal for a night in! You can pair it with pasta, rice or potatoes.

Ingredients Needed

  • 1 pound of boneless chicken breasts
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon of black pepper
  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil
  • 1 cup of chopped yellow onion
  • 3 cloves of garlic
  • 3/4 cup of chicken broth
  • 1/2 teaspoon of dried oregano
  • 1/4 teaspoon of crushed red pepper
  • 3/4 cup of whipping cream
  • 1/2 cup of grated parmesan cheese
  • 1 teaspoon of pepperoncini brine
  • 1/2 cup of sliced pepperoncini peppers
  • chopped fresh parsley

Directions

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Cut your chicken breasts into smaller cubes. Then season both sides with salt and pepper.
  3. Pour your olive oil into a large skillet over mediium heat. Place your chicken cubes into the skillet and cook for about 6 minutes or until chicken is lightly browned and no longer pink in the middle. Set cooked chicken aside.
  4. Add your onion to the skillet and cook for 2 minutes. Then add your garlic and cook for 1 minute. Finally, add your chicken broth, oregano, and crushed red pepper and let simmer for 2 minutes.
  5. Add and stir in whipping cream, parmesan, and pepperoncini brine to the skillet. Let it simmer and cook for 5 minutes or until the mixture is slighlty thickened.
  6. Add you chicken breasts back to the skillet and stir to coat. Then add your sliced pepperoncini. You can serve with parsley.
